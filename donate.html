What is Donato?

On our platform, you can donate/Get items with confidence. We do all this so that our donors can trust that their giving is indeed doing what it's supposed to do - change lives. Simple: Give at a click of a button Convenience: Contribute at any time with ease Donate with confidence to the verified partner NGOs.
