Project Overview

The project aims to bring car owners and mechanics together to prevent them from being stranded being stuck in the road and choosing their car service as per their convenience. This app provides 24*7 service as they can find nearby service centers, Auto spare parts, Showrooms etc and incase of any emergency and user can also track & instant connect with their help for getting repaired and ask for quotations. Also they can buy and sell the car or see information about any car related service online as per their need.

Problem Statement

Its a stress when car breaks down in the middle of road at any time or when you need any kind of car service as per your specific time/ place but you don't have any faster option.

  • Service Centers are irresponsible to deliver the vehicles in time which are given for service
  • Unavailability of towing vehicle most of the time
  • Busy in work, gets to no time to take vehicle to service Centre on its own
  • Vehicle are priced so don't trust all mechanics to handle It. Some seems unprofessional so difficult to trust.
  • User cannot buy or sell vehicles online

    • Solution Statement
  • App will provide 24/7 support even if it is breakdown at sudden circumstance
  • Services will be done by the specialized mechanics
  • Sell and buy option is available and it is made genuine
  • Spare parts are available to door of user location with taxes
  • Add business information for user to post in the timelines are provided to promote their own growth of the user
  • Buying or selling of vehicles with all documents and details re collected properly

    • Project goal

    Access to professional mechanics based on their specialisation. Any User having Car Service Garages or showrooms or spare part centers are eligible to register their own business, Give service and authentic spare parts at good price, Tracking and Chat help or car servicing, Incorporation of routine services and reminders on platfrom
